Frequently Asked Questions
What is NeuroMatrix Biosystems? ▼
NeuroMatrix Biosystems is a neuroscience research software company developed at the University of Ilorin, Nigeria. We build AI-powered tools for preclinical and clinical neuroscience research, with a focus on making world-class research software accessible to African researchers and institutions.
What is NeuroTrack Pro? ▼
NeuroTrack Pro is an AI-powered behavioral analysis platform for preclinical neuroscience. It automates animal tracking in the Morris Water Maze, Y-Maze, and Open Field Test — generating trajectory maps, heatmaps and quantitative metrics from standard lab videos. It is a direct affordable alternative to commercial software like ANY-maze.
Is NeuroTrack Pro free to use? ▼
NeuroTrack Pro has a free tier that allows up to 3 video sessions with basic trajectory analysis. Full features including heatmap, Y-Maze, Open Field Test, probe trial analysis and unlimited sessions require a one-time license starting from $29 — compared to $1,000–$5,000+ for commercial alternatives.
What camera do I need for NeuroTrack Pro? ▼
Any standard camera that can record video works — smartphone cameras, USB webcams, or dedicated lab cameras. NeuroTrack Pro accepts MP4, AVI, and MOV formats. No proprietary hardware is required, unlike commercial systems that bundle expensive camera setups.
Can I use NeuroTrack Pro for my PhD research? ▼
Yes — NeuroTrack Pro is designed specifically for academic research. The Researcher plan includes a software citation formatted for your methods section. The platform has been used in active PhD research at the University of Ilorin funded by TETFund and the GRASP/NIH/DSI Collaborative Program.
What is the African NeuroHealth AI Dashboard? ▼
African NeuroHealth AI is a clinical AI platform for predicting stroke and dementia risk in African populations. It incorporates culturally relevant factors including lifestyle, environmental stressors and dietary patterns. It achieves 86.7% stroke prediction accuracy and 93.3% dementia prediction accuracy, and is freely accessible online.
Who funds this research? ▼
Our research is supported by TETFund Nigeria under the Academic Staff Training & Development (AST&D) scheme, and the Growing Data-science Research in Africa to Stimulate Progress (GRASP) program — a collaboration between African institutions, the National Institutes of Health (NIH) and the Data Science Initiative (DSI).
How do I collaborate with NeuroMatrix Biosystems? ▼
We welcome collaborations with researchers, institutions and universities across Africa and globally. Fill in the contact form below with your research interests and institution. We are particularly interested in multi-site behavioral neuroscience studies, clinical AI validation and educational partnerships.
Is the software peer-reviewed? ▼
A manuscript describing NeuroTrack Pro is currently being prepared for submission to SoftwareX (Elsevier) — a peer-reviewed journal for research software. The African NeuroHealth AI Dashboard has been presented at international conferences and is actively used in ongoing NIH-supported research.
What is the NeuroMatrix LMS? ▼
The NeuroMatrix Learning Management System is an upcoming platform for neuroscience research training, designed for African universities and research institutions. It will provide structured course modules, interactive research methodology training, certificate programs and integration with our analysis tools. Contact us for early access.
