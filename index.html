Behavioral Analysis
NeuroTrack Pro

AI-powered Morris Water Maze, Y-Maze and Open Field Test analysis platform with real-time trajectory tracking and heatmap generation.

  • Video upload + auto rat tracking
  • Escape latency & path length metrics
  • Probe trial quadrant analysis
  • Y-Maze spontaneous alternation
  • OFT anxiety index + velocity
  • Desktop .exe + web version

Clinical AI
African NeuroHealth AI

Culturally-attuned stroke and dementia risk prediction platform incorporating lifestyle, environmental and psychosocial factors relevant to African populations.

  • Stroke risk prediction (86.7% accuracy)
  • Dementia risk assessment (93.3% accuracy)
  • Memory game cognitive training
  • Nutrition & stress assessment
  • English / French language support
  • PDF downloadable reports
