Frequently Asked Questions
What is included in the license? ▼
Your license unlocks full access to NeuroTrack Pro web app AND the desktop Windows .exe installer. It covers Morris Water Maze, Y-Maze, and Open Field Test analysis including video upload, auto-tracking, trajectory, heatmap, all metrics, PNG and CSV export.
How do I receive my license key? ▼
After payment via Paystack or bank transfer, your unique license key is emailed to you within 24 hours. For Paystack payments it is usually automated within minutes.
Is this a one-time payment or subscription? ▼
100% one-time payment — perpetual license with no recurring fees. Researcher and Institution plans include free upgrades for 1 year and lifetime respectively.
Can I use this for my PhD thesis? ▼
Yes! NeuroTrack Pro is designed specifically for academic research. The Researcher plan includes a software citation you can include in your methods section.
How does NeuroTrack Pro compare to ANY-maze? ▼
ANY-maze costs $1,000–$5,000+ and requires hardware bundles. NeuroTrack Pro starts at $29, works with any existing camera, runs on any Windows PC, and includes a web version — making it ideal for African research institutions with limited budgets.
What if I need a refund? ▼
We offer a 7-day refund policy if the software does not work as described. Contact [email protected] with your license key and reason.
